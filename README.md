# Draqun

[![github](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/Mopsgamer/draqun.svg?style=flat)](https://github.com/Mopsgamer/draqun)
[![github issues](https://img.shields.io/github/issues/Mopsgamer/draqun.svg?style=flat)](https://github.com/Mopsgamer/draqun/issues)

Draqun is a unified, modular ecosystem designed to eliminate platform
fragmentation. Instead of bouncing between dozens of disconnected apps for
project management, streaming, professional networking, and personal
communication, Draqun unifies them into a single, high-performance platform
built on top of a real-time communication core.

## The Vision: Why do this?

Modern digital tools are completely fragmented. Your team uses Slack for chat,
GitHub for tasks, Zoom for meetings, and Notion for documentation. Everyday
users bounce between WhatsApp, LinkedIn, and Twitch.

Draqun fixes this by putting everything in one place. Every feature is a module
connected directly to your core chat identity. Because chat is the foundation,
data moves between modules instantly - a task ticket can become a chat thread,
and a document can turn into a live presentation with one click.

## What Draqun Does (The Modules)

### Companies & Teams

- **Team Chat:** Centralized text and media rooms for internal teams.
- **Task Tracking:** Built-in issue tracking tied directly to your communication
  channels.
- **Hiring Pipeline:** Post job listings, collect portfolios, and manage
  candidate workflows.
- **Integrated Video:** Host internal meetings or interview candidates right
  inside the app.
- **Wiki & Knowledge Base:** Interconnected documentation and internal knowledge
  sharing.
- **Client Portals:** Dedicated, isolated spaces to share project progress and
  track bugs with clients.

### Creators, Streamers & Musicians

- **Community Hubs:** Custom spaces tailored for fan engagement.
- **Go-Live Alerts:** Instant push notifications to let followers know when you
  publish content.
- **Media Hosting:** Native hosting and streaming for videos, podcasts, and
  audio.
- **Business Tools:** Access to the same task tracking and scheduling tools used
  by companies.

### TV Channels & Broadcasters

- **Live Broadcasting:** High-bitrate video streaming with optional, low-latency
  live chat.
- **Scalable Infrastructure:** Full media management backend built to handle
  large-scale traffic.

### Regular Users

- **Unified Profile:** A single account for career networking, job hunting, and
  casual socializing.
- **Messaging:** Fast, high-quality text, voice, and video calls for friends and
  family.
- **Safe Networking:** Opt-in to community-moderated social spaces to meet
  people, strictly shielded from advertisers.
- **Local Events:** Discover real-life meetups, local workshops, or set up your
  own events.
- **Cloud Storage:** A personal vault to save and share files and documents.

### Schools & Universities

- **Availability Stats:** Public dashboards showing enrollment space and
  waitlists.
- **Portals:** Share public event calendars and timetables, while keeping
  classroom materials private.
- **Parent Channels:** Direct lines for teachers to communicate and call
  parents.

### Governments & Public Sector

- **Public Feeds:** Share announcements, official stats, new laws, or stream
  parliament sessions live.
- **Economic Boards:** Post civic investment opportunities and public sector job
  openings.

### Freelancers & Solopreneurs

- **Portfolios:** Showcase completed work, case studies, and services.
- **Client Management:** Manage leads, chat with clients, handle contracts, and
  track milestones.
- **Reviews:** Collect verified client reviews and answer them publicly.
